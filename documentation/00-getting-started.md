# Getting Started with YouTube on InfluenceIQ

Welcome! Connecting your YouTube channel takes about 2 minutes, and once it's done, InfluenceIQ starts pulling in all your data automatically.

---

## Connecting Your Channel

1. Go to the **YouTube** section in the sidebar.
2. You'll see a "Connect with Google" screen — click it.
3. Google will ask you to sign in and give InfluenceIQ permission to read your channel data.
4. After you approve, you'll be taken straight to your YouTube Overview dashboard.

That's it. No complicated setup, no API keys to manage on your end.

> **Heads up:** You need to connect the Google account that owns your YouTube channel. If you manage multiple channels under one Google account, we'll connect the primary one. Multi-channel support is coming soon.

---

## What Happens After You Connect

As soon as you connect, InfluenceIQ pulls in:

- Your **channel info** — name, subscriber count, video count
- Your **last 50 videos** — titles, thumbnails, view counts, likes
- **12 months of daily analytics** — views, watch time, subscribers gained and lost
- **Retention curves** for eligible videos (needs 1,000+ views per video)

The first sync takes about 30–60 seconds. You'll see your dashboard populate in real time.

---

## Keeping Your Data Fresh

Your data syncs **once a day automatically**, so your dashboard is always up to date by morning.

You can also trigger a manual sync any time by clicking the **Sync** button in the top-right corner of any YouTube page. This is useful right after you publish a new video or if you just want the freshest numbers.

---

## The Sync Button Explained

The Sync button pulls fresh data from YouTube's API:
- Latest video stats (views, likes, comments)
- New videos you've published since the last sync
- Updated daily analytics metrics

It doesn't re-analyze retention curves or re-run AI features — those run automatically in the background.

---

## What the Sidebar Shows

Once connected, you'll see these pages in the YouTube section:

| Page | Quick description |
|---|---|
| Overview | Your key numbers: total views, watch hours, subscriber growth |
| Retention Studio | AI explains why viewers drop off your videos |
| Outlier Radar | Alerts when a competitor posts a viral video |
| Predictive Studio | Predicts how your newest video will perform |
| Archive Miner | Finds old videos worth remaking or repurposing |
| Cross-Platform | Shows whether Instagram drives YouTube subscribers |

---

## Disconnecting

If you ever want to disconnect your YouTube channel, click **Disconnect YouTube** in the sidebar. This removes your channel data from InfluenceIQ. You can reconnect at any time.
