# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Instagram content creators (business/creator accounts) checking analytics after posting. Typically on desktop, scanning numbers quickly between content creation sessions. Goal: understand what's working, spot trends, act on data.

## Product Purpose

Multi-platform social analytics dashboard. Currently Instagram-only; YouTube and TikTok are planned. Stores data beyond Meta's 90-day window via ClickHouse. Gives creators long-term trend visibility that native Instagram Insights doesn't provide.

## Brand Personality

Precise, creator-warm, data-first. Not enterprise. Not playful. Confidence without coldness.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS cream: Inter everywhere, gradient hero-metric cards, purple on white with no opinion
- Heavy enterprise: gray tables, zero personality, zero brand presence
- Neon dark mode: glowing violet on near-black, gaming aesthetic

## Design Principles

1. Data first: every pixel serves the data, not the chrome
2. Compact by default: creators check stats fast; information density is a feature
3. Earned familiarity: use standard affordances so the tool disappears into the task
4. Platform-aware: Instagram now, expandable to YouTube and TikTok without a rewrite

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. No specific known accommodations beyond standard contrast and focus states.
